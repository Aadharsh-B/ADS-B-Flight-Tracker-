Hello! This Document presents to the user, the step-by-step instructions for displaying the Airnav Webpage (User Interface) loaded in a Raspberry-Pi onto an External Display

1. Open a Web Browser [for current implementation, the Chromium Browser is preferred]
HOW TO OPEN A WEB BROWSER?
	1. Click on the Raspberry-Pi icon on the Left-Top of the Scrren
	2. Hover on the "Internet" Tab and Select "Chromium Browser"
2. On Bookmarks i.e. the Left-Top of the New Blank Tab in the  Browser Window, the Link for a FEEDER STATION is available, Click on It
3. The "AIRNAV" webpage opens up.
4. Zoom Optimally
5. Click on the "FULLSCREEN" button in the Bottom-Right of the Web-Page

Congragulations! You have Successfully entered the page for viewing flights captured by your feeder station. View Flights! Enjoy!
